My Story

I have always known myself to be someone who is driven by creativity. I began my professional career as a music composer, performer, and educator. While I loved the art of performing on stage, I was always more drawn to composing. I loved the craft of creating pieces of music from scratch and the process of continually chipping away them as they evolve and come to life. I found a similar drive and passion in my career as a bartender. I Loved learning about the subtleties of the tools at my disposal and then using them to create custom craft cocktails and experiences for my clientele.

I made the jump to coding upon the trusted recommendation of a friend who was in the tech field. After exploring a few online courses, and gaining enough of a skill set to begin creating web content, I found myself sitting at my computer for upwards of 8 hours at a time experimenting and creating in much the same fashion and with the same curiosity that I had with music and spirits. I view web development as another empowering outlet for creativity and problem solving.

I envision myself working as a front end developer in a progressive environment with a team to produce creative and innovative solutions while always looking to push the boundaries of what is currently possible.

My Education

I am a proud graduate of Dev Bootcamp NYC. The Skills and tools I acquired at DBC have empowered me with the confidence to find creative solutions to virtually any design issue I encounter. During my time in bootcamp, I also refined my skills in positive communication and my ability to foster collaborative spaces in an empathetic and productive manner.

I am also a long time subscriber to educational sites like Treehouse, Lynda, and Egghead. An inherent drive to always be pushing my own boundaries and keep my code fresh keeps me in a constant state of hunger for knowledge. I have no foreseeable plans to take a break in my educational journey.
